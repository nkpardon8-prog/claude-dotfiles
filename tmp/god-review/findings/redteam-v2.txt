### [high] Newline-injection guard does NOT reject newlines on macOS bash 3.2.57
**Source:** redteam
**Evidence:** `god-review.md:979` and `1239` use `case "$ARCH_FILE" in *[\;\&\|\`\$\(\)\<\>\\]* | *$'\n'* | *$'\r'* | '') ... ;; esac`. The `$'\n'` C-string escape is bash 4+. ### [medium] `compute_finding_hash` line-range normalization rounds asymmetrically
**Source:** redteam
**Evidence:** `god-review.md:884-890, 1267-1273`. Formula: `print(f'{(s//5)*5}-{((e+4)//5)*5}')`. For `s=42, e=47`: bucket `(42//5)*5 = 40`, `((47+4)//5)*5 = (51//5)*5 = 50`. So `42-47` ‚Üí `40-50`. ### [minor] `cat ~/.claude-dotfiles/commands/god-review/broad-reviewers/codex-cross-layer.md` etc. use literal `~` inside Bash command-substitution
**Source:** redteam
**Evidence:** `god-review.md:571, 576, 581`. `cat ~/.claude-dotfiles/...` works because tilde-expansion happens.
